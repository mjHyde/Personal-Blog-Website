Summer Adventures

Hey there! My name is Morgan and one of my favorite things to do in the summer is hiking and exploring areas. I love using AllTrails app to look up interesting hikes in the areas I'm looking to explore.

I'm really lucky to live so close to several National Parks and I really have enjoyed going to explore them with family or by myself. I love rocks! and rock formations!
