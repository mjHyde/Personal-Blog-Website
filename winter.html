Winter Adventures
❄️

During the Winter I love snowboarding more than anything so I try to spend as much time up in the mountains as I can afford. I have boarded at a few mountians and some of my favorite are:

  1. Copper
  2. Wolf Creek
  3. Park City
  4. Vail

Snowboarding is not an easy or cheap sport to pick up. You have to be dedicated enough to keep getting up after each fall and persevere through the fear of trying something so unique. Being in the mountains with the snow is an expereince I hope everyone can enjoy at least once.
